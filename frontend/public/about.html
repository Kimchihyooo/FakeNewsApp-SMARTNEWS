User Guide

Master the tools of digital verification.

For Text Content

01

Copy the Content

Navigate to the news site or social media platform. Highlight the suspicious Philippine news article and copy it to your clipboard.

Ctrl + C
02

Paste into Detector

Go to the CredibilityScan Detector page. Click inside the text area and paste your content.

Ctrl + V
03

Analyze Content

Hit the Analyze button. The model will process the text checking for credibility patterns.

Click Analyze
04

View Results

Review the output. The system will flag the content as Real or Fake based on the calculated probability score. The system will also provide supporting evidences from reputable Philippine news sources.

For Video Content

01

Copy the Video URL

Copy to your clipboard the url of the suspicious Philippine Video News content from any platforms like YouTube, Tiktok, Facebook, Instagram, etc.

Ctrl + C
02

Paste into Detector

Go to the CredibilityScan Detector page. Click the Video tab and paste your video URL.

Ctrl + V
03

Analyze Content

Hit the Analyze Video button. The model will process the video if manipulated by Deepfake Technology and collects supporting evidences from Reputable Philippine News Sources.

Click Analyze
04

View Results

Review the output. The system will flag the Video content as Real or Fake based on the model's probability score. The system will also show the collected supporting evidences from reputable Philippine news sources.
