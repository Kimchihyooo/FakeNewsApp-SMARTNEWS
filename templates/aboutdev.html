How to Use

For Text Content

01

Copy the Content

Navigate to the news site or social media platform. Highlight the suspicious Philippine news article and copy it to your clipboard.

Ctrl + C
02

Paste into Detector

Go to the CredibilityScan Detector page. Click inside the text area and paste your content.

Ctrl + V
03

Analyze Content

Hit the Analyze button. The model will process the text checking for credibility patterns.

Click Analyze
04

View Results

Review the output. The system will flag the content as Real or Fake based on the calculated probability score. The system will also provide supporting evidences from reputable Philippine news sources.
