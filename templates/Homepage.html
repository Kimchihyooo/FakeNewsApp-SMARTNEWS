How SmartNews Works?

How SmartNews acts as your digital detective to spot fake news.

01. The Readers

Text Analysis

The Problem: Sometimes people write stories that aren't true.

The Solution: We have two digital assistants named XLM - RoBERTa and Tagalog RoBERTa. They read every single word in the blink of an eye to understand the "feeling" of the storyâ€”whether it sounds like real news or a made-up lie.

02. The Voting Team

Random Forest Model

The Problem: One opinion isn't enough.

The Solution: We use a Random Forest. Imagine a room filled with hundreds of tiny experts. Each one looks at a small piece of the story and votes: "Real" or "Fake". If the majority says "Fake," we warn you!

03. The Face Checker

Deepfake Detection

The Problem: Videos can be edited to make people say things they never said.

The Solution: We use YOLOv8 (The Spotter) to find faces and MobileNetv2 (The Inspector) to look at tiny pixels. If the skin looks blurry or the lighting is weird, we know it's a trick.

04. The Investigator

Metadata & Fact-Checking

The Problem: Fake news often hides behind misleading titles, false captions, or re-uploaded old footage.

The Solution: We use Automated Cross-Referencing. The system extracts the video's core metadata (title, tags, source) and instantly queries via Google Custom Search API capturing related news on Reputable Philippine news outlets. It acts like a digital detective, confirming if the event is verified by legitimate news outlets.

05. The Highlighter

Explainable AI (LIME)

The Problem: You need to know why something is fake.

The Solution: We use LIME. It's like a magic highlighter. We highlight suspicious sentences in Red, so you can see the proof yourself.

06. The Librarian

Cross-Referencing

The Problem: Is anyone else reporting this?

The Solution: We check the "Library" using Google Search. We look for your story on trusted news websites. If trusted sources are telling the same story, itâ€™s likely true.
